I am an artist based out of Austin, TX. I work with different mediums along with a myriad of digital processes to create work that explores hidden patterns and their behavior. I explore patterns that not only manifest visually but also through psychologial means. There are patterns in identity, thoughts, language, and everything that we interact with. I find that a central idea tends to reveal itself subconsciously when I am working on a project. Place, memory, the past, and language are subjects that frequently come up in my work.
