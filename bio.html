I am an artist based out of Austin, TX. I work with a myriad
of digital and traditional processes to create work that explores
hidden patterns and their behavior along with the character of
written language. I explore patterns that not only manifest
visually but also through psychological and societal means.
I find that a central idea tends to reveal itself subconsciously
when I am working on a project. Technology, memory, and language
are subjects that frequently come up in my work. I find the practice
of collage to be a reference to its origins among Victorian women.
I aim to highlight and recontextualize the practice of crafts among
housewives by using cut-out grids from cross stitch books and magazines.
The materials in my work are all repurposed either from past projects
or come from ephemera that would have otherwise been discarded.
