Ava Wadley is a visual artist from Austin, Texas who works with a myriad of mediums along with different digital processes and artifacts. Her work centers around technology and how what is synthetic can sometimes mesh together with what is natural. Her work also deals with the trans experience and communication. She works with digital text, fragmented poetry, and typed images brought together to ask questions about language and images. She has learned to code independently to further explore the relationship between art and programming. She enjoys building personal archives of online media including photos, digital video, and websites. She is involved with the DIY music scene in Austin, Texas, and performs as a DJ under the name "salon". She is equally as drawn to music as she is visual art and seeks to explore the music scene further.
